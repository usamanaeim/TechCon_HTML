History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small tech meetup and has grown into one of the most anticipated technology conferences worldwide.

Mission

TechCon aims to bring together innovators, developers, and tech leaders to shape the future of technology and collaboration.

Notable Past Speakers

  • Linus Torvalds – Creator of Linux, shared insights on open-source innovation.
  • Sheryl Sandberg – Former COO of Meta, discussed leadership and inclusion.
